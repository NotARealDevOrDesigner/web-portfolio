[Haptic] [2024]

demo & haptic in vr environments

Development of a VR test environment in Unity and Unreal Engine to simulate and evaluate haptic effects within company projects. The environment was designed for both internal testing and client demonstrations. These details should suffice, as the projects are internal.

bachelor thesis

haptic design & knowledge transfer

As part of a bachelor's thesis, a concept was developed to introduce UX students at Aalen University to the topic of haptics. Despite its high relevance in UX, haptics had not yet been established as a dedicated course topic. The project defined the content, scope, and method of knowledge transfer to create an effective and structured learning experience.

research & learning process

The Experiential Learning approach was used to effectively convey haptics, because talking about it is not enoughâ€”it must be experienced. Based on scientific principles and established findings, a series of progressively complex tasks was developed. These exercises guided students from fundamental haptic concepts to the final creation of vibrotactile feedback using a prototyping tool.

learnings

haptic design & challenges

During my time working on haptic feedback systems, I gained a deep understanding of haptic design and its technical implementation. I also became aware of the complexities and challenges associated with haptics. Thanks to the high level of expertise at Nuilab, I was able to learn a lot from my colleagues. It became clear how important precision and attention to detail are when creating a seamless and effective haptic user experience.

vr & hard/software

Through this work, I gained valuable experience with Unreal Engine, particularly in the integration of haptic effects in VR environments. I also developed skills in communication between hardware and software, including working with Arduino, which further enhanced the technical implementation of haptic feedback.

problem solving & mindset shift

It was through my time at Nuilab that I gained a new perspective on problem-solving, shifting the focus from "what" can be done to "how" to make it happen. This mindset also emphasized the importance of being flexible, adapting to new topics, learning new tools, and conducting experiments to validate assumptions. The knowledge and approach gained continue to influence my work and shape my problem-solving strategies.

Kiev 60 L-Grip

Rethink soviet design and outdated ergonomics

problem

handling & usability

At nearly 2 kg, the Kiev 60 is bulky and uncomfortable for handheld use. Unlike other medium format cameras of its time, it lacked L-grip options to improve handling. This design oversight makes extended shooting a challenge —one that calls for a better solution.

design

holding positions

ergonomics & aesthetics

The L-grip is designed to enhance the Kiev 60's usability while maintaining its iconic aesthetic. The grip's contours are carefully crafted to fit the hand, providing a comfortable and secure hold. The design is minimalistic, ensuring that it complements the camera's vintage look without overwhelming it.

braighausen

Website and corporate Design for an Event Location

[UI Design] [2023]

idea

redesign & usability

A fresh take on an existing presence. The site was redesigned to reflect the character of the location—informing regulars about events, while inviting new clients to discover Braighausen as a venue for weddings and more.

elements

design

identity & basic design

The goal was to create a clear and welcoming digital space that reflects Braighausen’s atmosphere while building a subtle sense of identity. The design balances calm aesthetics with strong usability—aimed to work equally well for new visitors and older audiences. Realized in Webflow for flexible, hands-on management.

learnings

seo optimization

During the Braighausen project, I developed a deeper understanding of SEO strategies and their practical application. I focused on optimizing the site’s structure, using relevant keywords, and improving load speed to enhance both visibility and user experience. The project highlighted the importance of balancing technical performance with content relevance, ensuring the website ranks well while meeting user needs.

figma & webflow workflow

I learned how to work efficiently with Figma and tools like Webflow. A key takeaway was how to structure work in Figma to ensure a seamless handoff to development. I also refreshed my programming skills and deepened my understanding of web development fundamentals, enabling me to bring design concepts to life in a functional website.

client & communication

I learned how to effectively gather client requirements and collaborate throughout the design process. It wasn't just about listening to their needs but actively thinking along with them to create solutions that balanced their vision with technical feasibility. The close communication and iterative approach helped deliver a solution that exceeded expectations.

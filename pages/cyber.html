story & gamemechanics

Cyberescape 2077 is a simple VR escape gamedemo set in a utopian cybercity. Players must evade patrolling drones while securing an energy cell, using mechanics like grabbing objects, using a weapon, and unlocking doors with a keycard. A stealth system allows for strategic movement to avoid detection.

mapdesign

logic & assets

The game uses immersive assets like modular map elements to create a believable world. The drone AI and player stealth mechanics were carefully designed to ensure responsive behavior and a challenging but fair gameplay experience.

tools & integration

Working with Blender, Unity, and VR integration deepened my understanding of asset creation, scene building, and implementing interactive elements using the programming language C#.

vr development

Through this process, I learned how to recreate specific properties, such as selecting the right leather or finding materials with similar optical qualities but reduced weight. I also experimented with various surface treatments, including lacquer and powder coating, to achieve the desired finish and durability while maintaining the aesthetic integrity of the design.

immersion & new perspective

I learned how crucial immersion is in game design and how mechanics, flow, and interaction shape the player’s experience. Developing this game allowed me to approach design from a new perspective, focusing on presence and engagement in a fully interactive world.
